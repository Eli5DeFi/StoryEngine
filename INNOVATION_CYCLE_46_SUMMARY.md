**Revenue:** $10K (Year 1) ‚Üí $5.7M (Year 3) ‚Üí $50M (Year 5)  
**Moat:** 60 months

**Examples:**
- Senate destroyed in Voidborne ‚Üí Affects all multiverse stories
- Own "Zara SBT" ‚Üí She appears in 10 different stories
- Writers earn 10% royalty when others reference their story

### 4. Dynamic Difficulty Adjustment (DDA) ‚öñÔ∏è
**Problem:** Newbies always lose, pros always win, poor retention  
**Solution:** Personalized odds based on Elo rating

**How:** Track prediction accuracy ‚Üí Adjust odds (+20% for newbies, -20% for pros)  
**Impact:** +40% retention, fair onboarding, skill-based matchmaking  
**Revenue:** $50K (Year 1) ‚Üí $2.1M (Year 3) ‚Üí $10M (Year 5)  
**Moat:** 30 months

**Tiers:**
- Newbie (Elo 0-800): Protected pool, bonus odds
- Intermediate (800-1200): Normal odds
- Advanced (1200-1600): Reduced odds
- Grandmaster (1600+): High stakes, no handicaps
