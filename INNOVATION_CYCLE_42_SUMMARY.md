### 3. Character Reputation NFTs
**The Problem:** Can't own or influence favorite characters.

**The Solution:** Mint character NFTs. Holders vote on character decisions, unlock backstory, earn royalties when characters appear.

**Impact:**
- 50x retention (collectors hold for months/years)

### 4. Story Forking Protocol
**The Problem:** Single timeline. Losing bettors feel cheated.

**The Solution:** DAO governance lets community fork the story at any chapter, creating alternate timelines via crowdfunding.

**Impact:**
- Infinite content (community-driven)
- Losing bettors can fund their preferred outcome
- Each fork has own betting pools + NFTs

**Revenue:** $62.5K/year  
**Difficulty:** Hard (3-4 weeks)

### 5. Sentiment Oracle Network
**The Problem:** No privacy. Whales can't bet without moving markets.

**The Solution:** Zero-knowledge proofs enable private betting + public sentiment aggregation.

**Impact:**
- 10x whale participation (>$5K bets)
- Privacy without sacrificing transparency
- Real-time sentiment feed for AI agents

**Revenue:** $85K/year  
**Difficulty:** Very Hard (4-6 weeks)
